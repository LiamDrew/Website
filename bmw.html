BMW Ownership & Auto Repair: The E46 Experience

The 1999-2005 E46 generation is one of the most legendary BMW 3 series generations, during which BMW sold 2.6 millions 3 series cars. These cars are numerous and surprisingly reliable (at least compared to newer BMWs). You can find a decent example on Facebook Marketplace for a few grand, making them a great starter car for new enthusiasts.
